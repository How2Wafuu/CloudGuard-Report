# Target Groups

CloudGuard AI is specifically designed to protect Small and Midsize Businesses (SMBs) and Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). These organizations form the backbone of the economy but have historically been the most underserved demographic in the cybersecurity market.

### The Market Landscape

Based on our market analysis—particularly focused on the Southeast Asian and Thai SME sectors—our target demographic faces a unique set of challenges

* **Severe Budget Constraints:** The average annual IT budget for an SME is tightly restricted (often around 1 million THB), with 60% of SMEs allocating less than 5% of their IT budget to cybersecurity \[1].
* **The Talent Shortage:** There is a massive shortage of skilled cybersecurity professionals, with an estimated deficit of 30,000 professionals in Thailand alone \[1]. Most SMBs only have a handful of general IT staff and cannot afford a dedicated 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) team.
* **Economic Vulnerability:** There are over 3.1 million active SMEs operating in Thailand, accounting for 35% of the country's GDP \[3]. Leaving these businesses vulnerable to cyber threats presents a massive economic risk at the national level.
* **The "Lose-Lose" Scenario:** Our target users are currently trapped between two bad options:
  * _The Paid Trap:_ Premium enterprise SIEMs use volume-based licensing that punishes SMBs for experiencing high traffic.
  * _The Free Trap:_ Open-source SIEMs are technically free but require massive, expensive hardware and highly trained engineers to deploy and maintain locally.

### Our Target Personas and Deployment Tiers

To capture our Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)—SMBs needing automated threat detection without a dedicated SOC—CloudGuard AI targets two specific organizational profiles:

#### 1. Budget-Conscious IT Teams (Tier 1: CloudGuard Core)

**The Profile:** Companies with a small internal IT team that understands basic network management but lacks deep cybersecurity specialization. **What They Need:** A self-service platform. They need full Edge and HQ AI protection with automated network scanning (Agent Discovery) that they can manage themselves without enterprise-level licensing fees.

#### 2. Organizations Needing White-Glove Support (Tier 2: CloudGuard Managed)

**The Profile:** Businesses with little to no internal IT staff, or those handling sensitive data that require guaranteed 24/7 oversight. **What They Need:** A fully managed SOC system that operates daily so they don't have to. Our specialized team handles deployment and the two-week network baselining phase, and provides 24/7 incident response and automated containment (e.g., isolating infected hosts before ransomware spreads).
